Poster Presentation

Introduction

Rules

  1. As per your requirement you can take any standard size (e.g. A2) of sheet for making poster.
  2. Students should report 10 minutes before the event & should bring their receipt & i-card along with them.
  3. Judges decision will be final.
  4. Spot entries may or may not be available (conditions apply).
  5. The participants should bring valid ID cards of their respective colleges.

Structure

Round 1:

  1. There is only a single round.
  2. The topics can be technical or non-technical according to your choice.
  3. Suggested topics are e.g. technical topics like zero energy consumption building, 4G spectrum, recent researches etc and Non-technical topics like save girl child, eradication of illiteracy etc.

Register

  1. Entry Fees:
    • Individual: Rs.100/-
    • Per Group(Max 2 Members): Rs.150/-
  2. Complete your registration and confirm participation.
  3. You may register for multiple events.
    1. Please pay cash at the GCOEJ Info Desk in your college.
      2. OR
    2. at GCOEJ, prior to the event in case of online registrations.
      3. OR
    3. On the Spot Registration if Entries are not full.
