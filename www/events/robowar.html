Robo War

Introduction

Get ready to feel the chills and shivers down your spine. Make your arms strong to fight the battle . You are the warrior here. The robo warrior! Establish your strength with your weapon on the arena.

Rules

  1. A team can consist of maximum 4 members.
  2. They can be from same or different institutions.
  3. All teams members who wish to participate should carry their institute ID card.
Machine Specifications:
  • The machine should fit within a box of dimensions 50*50*70(L*B*H) cm.
  • The Machine should not weight more than 15 Kg.
  • Both wireless and wired machines are allowed.
  • Participants will be allowed to use maximum 24 Volt DC power supply.
Weapons Systems:
  • Robots can have any kind of cutters, flippers, saws, lifting devices, spinning drum, spinning hammers etc.
Conditions:
  • All robots must be easily visible and have controlled mobility in order to compete.
Battery and Power:
  1. Supply will be provided Voltage limit is 24 V. Excesses voltage supply will not allow.
  2. The machine must be powered electrically only.
  3. Use of IC engine is not Allowed.
  4. On board Batteries must be sealed, immobilized-electrolyte types.
  5. Use of damaged, non-leak proof batteries may lead to disqualification.
Pneumatics:
  1. Robot can use pressurized non-inflammable gases to actuate pneumatic devices. Maximum allowed outlet nozzle pressure is 8 bar.
  2. Participants must be able to indicate the used pressure with integrated or temporarily fitted pressure gauge. Also there should be provision to check the cylinder pressure on the bot.
  3. All pneumatic components on board a robot must be securely mounted. Care must be taken while mounting the pressure vessel and armor to ensure that if ruptured it will not escape the robot. The terms 'pressure vessel, bottle, and source tank' are used interchangeably.
Hydraulics:
  1. Robot can use non-inflammable liquid to actuate hydraulic devices e.g. cylinders.
  2. All hydraulic components on-board a robot must be securely mounted. Special care must be taken while mounting pump, accumulator and armor to ensure that if ruptured direct fluid streams will not escape the robot.
  3. All hydraulic liquids are required to be non-corrosive and your device should be leak proof. Maximum allowed pressure is 8 bars.
NOT ALLOWED:
  • Circuit jammers.
  • Chemicals, compressed gases &rockets, fuels, combustion power methods.
  • Fire, water weapons and magnets.
  • Use of I.C. Engine in any sense.
  • Extension of the robot is not allowed during the game play.
  • Jumping and hopping, Flying (airfoil using, helium balloons, hovercrafts etc.)
  • Fire equipmentâ€™s are restricted.
  • Change of battery will not be allowed during the match.

Structure

Judging Criteria:

  1. Each match is played in 1 to 1 format.
  2. Match time: 5 min
  3. Points distribution:
    • Attack =10 points
    • Strategies=10 points
    • Defence=10 points
    • Damages=20 points
  4. Any robot is immobile for 10 sec is disqualified.
