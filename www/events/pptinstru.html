Paper Presentation

Rules
  • Team Size- Maximum 2 candidates are allowed.
  • Send your full length paper at our respective e-mail id given in Contact Us TAB below.
  • Registration Fees - Rs. 300/- per team(Max 2 members) and Rs.150/- per individual, for those registering online their DD should reach the college only then candidate will be considered..
  • No refund will be offered under any circumstances, once registration for the event is done.
  • Paper must be in IEEE format IN TWO COLUMNS.
  • Abstract should not exceed 200 words and Full length Paper should not exceed 7 pages.
  • Details of IEEE format are as follows
    • Your paper must be in two column format with a space of 4.22mm (0.17") between columns.
    • The entire document should be in Times New Roman or Times font.
    • Font size-
      • Heading -14 pt
      • Sub-.Heading -12 pt
      • Text -10 pt
    • Title of paper,Names of authors & Institute name is clearly mentioned.
  • Abstract and the paper must be sent as different attachments.[Full paper will be required]
  • Please bring your PowerPoint presentation on a pen drive AND on a CD.
  • Soft-copies of the submitted paper and PowerPoint presentations must be in the 2007 format.
  • Please bring 3 hardcopies of the submitted paper on the day of the event.
  • The teams will get 8+2 minutes for presentation followed by question and answer session.
  • Participants must possess his/her college ID & receipt at the time of event otherwise entry will be denied.
  • The decision of judges and event heads will be final and binding.
