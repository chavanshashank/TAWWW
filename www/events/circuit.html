Circuit Frenzy

Introduction

As the name CIRCUIT FRENZY ,this event is related with technology and electronics circuits having lots of fun by providing you great potential. Resistors, capacitors, transistors, IC’s are what you dream at night! If Silicon Valley is your dream destination!! Here comes Circuit to trigger your pulse and open your “Technical Mind”.Experience the thrill of the circuit with your enthusiastic participation. So, come and deal with the circuits which will build your confidence with your technical knowledge.

Rules

  1. The maximum number of participants in a team will be two.
  2. Participants bear the whole responsibility of their components and Equipment and will be fine if damaged.
    3. Scoring rules:
  3. The first position will be secured by the team who clears all the bugs within the circuit in the least time.
  4. Points will be awarded during the event.
  5. If no team clears all the errors within the allotted time. The first position will be given to the team who scores the maximum points.
  6. The participants must report every half an hour, to the concerned person about the bugs cleared. 5 points will be awarded, if the cleared bug is correct and 3 points will be deducted, if it’s wrong. The total points secured by each team will be calculated at the end of session and prizes will be awarded accordingly.

Structure

  1. The competition will comprise of three rounds.
  2. Round 1 :( Elimination Round) This round will include multiple choice question test.[30 question for 30 mins].Questions will be based on “ELECTRONICS FUNDAMENTAL & CIRCUIT”.
  3. Round 2: (Intermediate round)
    • Part A: Surprise round for the participants.
    • Part B: This round consists of debugging of the circuit.
  4. Round 3: (Final Round) Participants have to solve 1 problem based on circuit building and debugging using CRO.
