Hospitality

On behalf of the entire Techno-Arena 2015 team, we would like to extend a warm welcome to one and all arriving to Jalgaon for this Technical festival. We hope that you enjoy every moment that you spend in GCOEJ. Technoarena consists of the programs like technical Paper/Poster Presentations contest for all disciplines and design activities like Robot Building, Product/Project Competition, Exhibition, etc. This year we are again organising our national level technical event. It aims at knowledge sharing, enhancement in interactions amongst technical student community. The activities arranged so that they enhance creativity and imagination power of the students. It is our pleasure to invite you to participate in Technoarena 2015.

We invite you to share our vision "Globally Accepted Engineers with Human Skills" and be a part of what promises to carry forth the legacy of the fantastic grip of the human mind. Join us Technoarena 2015, and experience the thrill in Arena. At the Hospitality team, we shall take every effort to ensure that your stay with us is comfortable and enjoyable.



Note : Admission strictly on first-come-first-serve basis.
