Town Creators

Introduction

To give an idea about overview of civil engineering and planning forming to develop smart city or town on area of sheet. The participants should have to draw a layout smart city or town as per the byelaws prescribed by municipal corporation or pwd.

Rules

  1. Maximum two members/participants are allowed in each group.
  2. Duration of this event is 3 hours.
  3. The participant should have to draw the layout of town as per requirement and function of city.
  4. The event organizer gives some content or requires places in town, the participant should draw at least minimum content to fulfill the requirement of smart town.
  5. Event organizers provides drawing sheets, pencils, eraser, sharpener etc. and other accessories such as scale, drafter & color pencil will not be provided.
  6. As per your representation judges will decide the winners & runner-up.

Structure

  1. Event will be started at 10:00 am.
  2. There is no any round will be conducted.
  3. Winning criteria will be depends your presentation.

Register

    • Individual: Rs.150/-
    • Per Group(Max 2 Members): Rs.250/-
  2. Complete your registration and confirm participation.
  3. You may register for multiple events.
    1. Please pay cash at the GCOEJ Info Desk in your college.
      2. OR
    2. at GCOEJ, prior to the event in case of online registrations.
      3. OR
    3. On the Spot Registration if Entries are not full.
